BMX For All

Come to Vicious Cycles and discover for yourself our quality bikes that can't be beat. Experience for yourself our uniquely individualized approach to service and customization that sets us apart from the rest. Here at Vicious Cycles, Chapel Hill's premier BMX specialty shop, we approach each person's relationship with their bike from a place of connection and passion. That means we guarantee that we will help you find your perfect match, not just a beautiful machine, but one that expresses who you are and what BMX means to you. We are fully committed to our mission of providing premier quality BMX bikes and service to everyone who loves BMX. And we never ever stop working towards our goal of a growing, vibrant and connected BMX community in the Triangle by providing bikes that inspire and nurture.

Everyone can and should love BMX, and we are here to make that a reality. We strongly believe in the connection between every rider and their bike. We believe in and respect the individual. We believe every bike has a story, and we want to hear it! At Vicious Cycles, these unique values mean that every single project is a unique labor of love. Let's build something beautiful together.
